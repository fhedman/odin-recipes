Gambas Pil Pil

Garlic and Chili Prawns

Recipe by Flawless Food

Ingredients

  • 180 grams prawns
  • 90 ml olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 chili pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Heat oil over high heat
  2. Add prawns and cook on one side for 2 minutes
  3. Add garlic chili and paprika
  4. Flip prawns and cook for 2 minutes
  5. Transfer the prawns and chili oil to a serving dish
  6. Season with parsley, salt and pepper
